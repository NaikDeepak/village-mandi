Apna Khet – Pre-book Mangoes Directly from Konkan Farmers

Direct farmer to consumer. No middlemen. Fresh from Farm.

What is Apna Khet?

Reconnect with the hands that feed you. Apna Khet allows you to pre-book farm produce directly from farmers in the Konkan region.

We facilitate advance commitment before harvest to ensure farmers get fair prices and you get authentic, chemical-free produce.

How it works

  1. Open Batch: You pre-book with a small advance (10% commitment fee) during the booking window.
  2. Closed Batch: Orders are locked and sent to farmers for harvest.
  3. Collection: Fresh produce is harvested and sourced significantly from Karnataka & Maharashtra regions.
  4. Delivery: Sorted and delivered to collection points in Pune/Mumbai.

Why trust us?

My father, Ramesh, is a farmer’s son from Ghotage. We realized that good intentions don't protect farmers—commitment does. By booking in advance, you enable farmers to plan with confidence.
